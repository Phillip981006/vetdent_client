About Us

Vetdent International is the brainchild of Gerhard Steenkamp who is currently the only director of the company.

Gerhard completed his BVSc degree in 1994 from the University of Pretoria’s Faculty of Veterinary Science at Onderstepoort, after obtaining his BSc (Zoology) at the same institution. He completed the inaugural course in dentistry offered through the European school for Advanced Veterinary Studies in 1998. After 3 years in Europe he returned to Onderstepoort where he has been the head of the Dentistry and Maxillofacial Surgery clinic ever since. Gerhard did his MSc on the clinically relevant morphometrics of African elephant tusks and in 2017 completed his PhD on structure, development and pathology of the cheetah maxillofacial complex.

In 2017 Gerhard became a founding member of the certificate in Zoo and Wildlife Dentistry under the auspices of the American Veterinary Dental College. His research interests include developmental dentistry and maxillofacial surgery of non-domesticated species as well as maxillofacial pathology and surgery of domestic species. He has lectured in many parts of North America, Europe, Asia, Australia and Africa. Gerhard has published 50 peer reviewed papers, authored or co-authored 5 book chapters and has delivered more than 50 international conference presentations.

Sevices

Through the years I have learnt that although exciting to work on these magnificent animals, it is better to try and prevent dental and maxillofacial disease and pathology, rather than treating it.

Elephants and other tusked mammals

I offer a comprehensive evaluation of the housing, diet and teeth of these animals. Clients are sent an annual report on the health of the animals’ teeth and any changes are discussed. This follows more of a heard health approach and clients pay an annual fee.

Consultations on all species

Consultations for individual animals with dental and maxillofacial pathology. Clients that have enrolled for an annual subscription will get the first 5 consultations (different conditions or different animals) as part of the annual fee. All other clients will be charged per consultation.

Treatment of patients

Treatments are discussed per case during the consultation and will be charged individually regardless of an annual subscriber or not.

  • Does Prof. Steenkamp only work on elephants?

    No, I work on all types of animals. I have a special interest in tusks and have spent the last 25 years improving the health of elephant tusks and molar teeth.

  • Can you give advice on the dentition of animals that do not have tusks?

    Yes, I can and will help you gladly.

  • Do you need a site visit to give advice?

    Depending on the type of advice you need, a site visit may not be needed. I can assist in many circumstances based on history, clinical signs, radiographs, CT scans, photographs or videos.
